DEBUNK

Debunk is an online magazine that showcases women of colour owning their style and confidence.

Debunk was founded upon a curious girl who craves exchanging stories and knowing people of all different walks of life. The aim is to expose our take on the world as women of colour in a pre-dominantly white society. It's no secret that there is a lack of diversity and inclusvity in the mainstream media, so the mission is to bring light to the underrepresented individuals.


Print magazine is reaching its end, and so, I have used my skills of web-developing to produce an online platform that will hopefully not disappoint. Debunk intends to offer inspiring stories, aesthetically pleasing and thought provoking content and hopefully, a lot fun and laughter.
